How to delete your account

  1. Open the purpose app
  2. Go to Account by tapping your profile → More Settings
  3. Select “Delete Account”

What happens after deletion

Your account data will be permanently deleted or anonymized within a reasonable timeframe. Some limited records may be retained temporarily where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention obligations.
