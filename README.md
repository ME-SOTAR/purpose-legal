# purpose Legal & Account Information

This repository hosts the official legal and account-management pages for the **purpose** mobile application.

## Included pages

- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Account Deletion Instructions

These pages are publicly accessible and linked inside the purpose mobile app and app store listings to comply with platform requirements (Google Play and Apple App Store).

## About purpose

purpose is an AI-powered nutrition and wellness app that helps users log meals, track hydration and mood, and receive personalized insights based on their data.

## Data & Account Deletion

Users can delete their account directly from within the app.  
When an account is deleted:

- Personal data is permanently deleted or anonymized within a reasonable timeframe
- Deleted data cannot be recovered
- Some limited records may be retained where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention obligations
